GUIDE

Quick Start Platform Setup

Welcome to LuminaPaint Admin. Follow these 3 steps to configure your studio environment:

  1. Import Paint Brand Libraries: Navigate to Color Library to populate paint collections from Sherwin-Williams or Dulux.
  2. Upload Texture Maps: Add seamless tileable texture pattern images under Textures & Surfaces for plaster or marble wall finishes.
  3. Invite Team Members: Go to Users and send invitation emails with assigned admin or designer roles.
FAQS

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply wall textures onto canvas selections?

Select any wall area using Lasso or Magic Wand in the Canvas Editor, open the right-side Texture Library panel, and click your desired texture swatch.

What photo resolutions are recommended for Room Templates?

For optimal visualization, upload JPEG or PNG photos at 1920x1080 resolution or higher (under 10MB file size).

How are user roles enforced?

Super Admins have full access to billing, platform settings, and user management. Workspace Admins can edit projects and upload room templates.
